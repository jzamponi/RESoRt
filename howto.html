How to use RESoRt?


Step 1: Choose calculator mode

Under Select your calculator mode, choose one of the options: Compute radio flux allows to calculate the radio flux at a selectable frequency for a given star formation rate. The modus Compute star formation rate allows to compute the SFR for a given radio flux.

Step 2: Choose source

Under Select your source once can either slect New source or some exemplary galaxies which have also been discussed in Schober et al. 2017. In the case of New source all input parameters need to be filled from scratch. When one of the exemplary normal star-forming and starburst galaxies is selected, all galaxy parameters are filled in automatically. If desired, these parameters can, however, also be adjusted manually.

Step 3: Fill in or ordjust galaxy parameters

Under Galaxy parameters one can enter ones favourite galaxy model. All parameters are labeled and unit are shown on the right hand side. Please check the glossary section for more information on the various parameters.
